How you can use our app?

Step 1. Connect with facebook

First, you will have to enter our application using one of your used social-sites (like facebook).

Step 2. Register to a ring(city group)

After your connection, you can join a city group by choosing a one city from the list, and then pressing the "SUBMIT" button. You can join any number of groups you desire!

Step 3. Start Chatting!

Only after the registration process (otherwise, you will not see any messages), you can join the city group and start chatting!!!
